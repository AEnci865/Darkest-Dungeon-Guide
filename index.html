Darkest Dungeon — Expedition Intelligence System
The Ruins
Holy Blight
Undead enemies resist Bleed. Prioritize Holy damage and Blight for Skeletons. Stress generation is moderate but constant.

Region Intelligence

  • Undead have 0% bleed resist — wait, they're IMMUNE. Use Blight & Holy exclusively.
  • Courtyard rooms often have Pestilent Courtyard events — bring extra antivenom.
  • Skeletons can self-resurrect once. Overkill with blight DoT to prevent this.
  • Bone Rabble should be cleared fast — they swarm and can stun-lock backliners.
  • Ancestor Shade patrols can spawn high-difficulty encounters in early game.
The Weald
Bleed Mark Prot Strip
Enemies often have high protection. Brigands hit hard from any position. Fungal enemies cause disease — bring more food.

Region Intelligence

  • Brigand Matchman — kill before he summons reinforcements. Highest priority target.
  • Fungal Scratcher causes Infected — food consumption +33%. Bring 8–10 food on medium.
  • Stress healer (Jester/Vestal) critical — Weald has many surprise stress-dealing combats.
  • Webber enemies can shuffle party positions — have positional flexibility.
  • Bleed + Mark synergy: BH marks, then Hellion shreds with If It Bleeds for massive output.
The Warrens
Bleed Blight
Beast-type enemies bleed well. Disease vectors are everywhere. High stress generation from pig-men affronts.

Region Intelligence

  • Swine enemies are all Beast-type — Houndmaster's Mark and Cry trigger bonus damage.
  • Disease is rampant — PD's Battlefield Medicine is a must or bring stacks of antivenom.
  • Carrion Eaters spawn from corpses — use abilities that prevent corpse creation.
  • Swinetaur Commanders can generate reinforcements — kill them first.
  • Pack in shorter dungeons to minimize disease accumulation. Short = 3 rooms ideal.
The Cove
Pierce Eldritch
Eldritch enemies are resistant to Bleed and Blight. High bleed-resist monsters. Shuffle abilities common — maintain position flexibility.

Region Intelligence

  • Cove enemies have 40–60% bleed resist — do NOT rely on bleed DoT here.
  • Squiffy Ghast applies massive stress — your Vestal must be on stress-heal watch.
  • Deep Dweller shamblers can summon The Shambler — never ignore curios here without torch.
  • Shuffle is constant — bring at least 2 heroes with positional flexibility (HWM, Hellion).
  • Shieldbreaker's Pierce bypasses the Cove's common high-protection enemy variants.
